Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Whether you are looking to improve your understanding of fundamentals, or gain that extra knowledge to achieve the grade you want – Login2Learns innovative system can provide you with the resources you need to further your skills in the build up to your exams.

On enrolment to Login2Learn, you will be asked to fill out our screening form. This will allow us to provide you with a tailored study plan to suit your need. You will be given access to the video tutorials across our platform in your chosen subject. Each video tutorial will be accompanied by exercise sheets for the student to attempt. Each video tutorial will also be closely linked with exam question solutions – creating a strong link between fundamental knowledge and the application of this to an exam style problem.
