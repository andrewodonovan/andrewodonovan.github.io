Who We Are

Login2Learn

Login2Learn, established in 2020, is one of Ireland’s most accessible services for online tutoring for Secondary level students.

Founded by Calum O’Brien, a mechanical engineer & experienced grinds teacher, Login2Learn’s initial aim was to provide effective online tutoring during the Covid-19 pandemic for students who Calum had already been working with.

Since then, our aim has changed – as we hope to provide easily-accessible learning support to all those who require it.

Through experience and student feedback, we have devised study aids for each curriculum which guide our students in exam-focused learning.

We look forward to helping you improve your skills, and ultimately find exam success.
